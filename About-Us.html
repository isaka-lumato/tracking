About Us

The Company

We are Diamond tracking System (DTS) company, a leading solutions provider in the area of GPS tracking system. Since founded, we have been on a mission to pursue excellence in vehicle and other assets tracking field. This system can help in fleet managment and finding  and getting back a lost asset.

Our Mission

 Our mission is to pursue excellence in the vehicle tracking field, Aiming at sound and stable development.
Today, we use the most advanced technologies for GPS devices, mobile and computer softwares which have been pervasively used in vehicles, personal tracking and other business operations. We work strictly in accordance with the international quality standard and our services and products are truly certified.
You are welcome to work with us!

Our Vision

Our vision is to became the most successful and respected vehicle tracking company in Easr Africa. 
We established office in Dar-es-salaam and we shall expand to an international level, covering more than 3 countries and regions.

What we do

We provide a live tracking system for assets like vehicles, motocycles, tvs, computer, transit containers, yellow plant equipment and more in affordable price.
